Banana Split

Ingredients

Breyers Ice Cream

Whipped Cream

Banana

Cherries

Chocolate Syrup

Strawberries (Optional)

1

Cut Da Banana

Peel the banana. Slice lengthwise into two pieces. Place slices into bowl.

2

Scoop Da Cream

Take a scoop, place it in a cup full of hot water. Then scoop da cream.

3

Whip Da Cream

Take the whip cream, place it on all three to four scoops of icecream.

4

Scoop Da Cherry

Take a spoon, get a jar of cherries. Scoop two to four cherries.

5

Pour Da Syrup

Take a bottle of Hershey's syrup, spread it over.

6

Eat Da Split

Get a friend, grab a spoon, and dig in.
